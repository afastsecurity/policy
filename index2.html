User Experience Program Statement

Welcome to the Fast Security User Experience Program!

In order to enhance your user experience of Fast Security and our services, we develop the User Experience Program. In this program, we will conduct statistical analysis of how users interact with the product and each of its functions. Through this analysis, we are able to continuously optimize your user experience and the performance of Fast Security, as well as to improve functionality and introduce innovative security features that benefit users. This User Experience Program will never collect users’ personal data and you can take part in this totally obey your own will.

Fast Security consider your privacy is significant and that’s why we provide this statement to explain the User Experience Program.


What types of information we collect:

  1. 1. Feature usage statistics during A/B Test:

 Statistics during A/B Test period about how you use Fast Security, including what the actions you take when using the app (such as buttons you click on in the app), how frequently you use a certain feature, to what extent a feature is being used, and so on. 

  1. 2. Basic application experience statistics: 

In order to attentively respond to the development of new malware and viruses on different mobile platforms, as well as to maximize satisfaction of primary user needs, we strive to continue to improve our product functionality. This is why we collect statistics on the use of each function; how the app behaves in consider of its performance, such as how long the junk is scanned, how much junk is scanned and what is being scanned. This represents the number of users for a particular function. 

  1. 3. Non-personal information:

we will collect your non-personal information, including your device information, phone model, logs, operation system, Android ID, CPN model, memory size, your phone IMEI number, rom, installed application name and package name, app md5 value, location, install, uninstall, frequency of use, and so on.

How your data is used:

Data collected only for the purpose of evaluating, analyzing, reviewing and improving the user experience of Fast Security. We will upload your installed apps information to  https://sla-intl.trustlook.com/v2/query to save for analysis. This address is our cooperate website and we will not use this information for other commercial purposes, unless we obtain your consent.

Data help us to more accurately determine the importance of each function, and guides us in adjusting its priority for research and development. During this process, we only analyze the usage frequency statistics of product functions, and this will never involve users’ personal data.


The security of the data:

   Fast Security strictly obeyed by and complies with relevant laws and regulations. The data collected by User Experience Program are completely anonymous. We will never match and store collected information against other personal information. Information is only collected as a means to enhance the service quality and efficiency that Fast Security provides to users.
